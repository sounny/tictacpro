How Eclipse Works

  • • Take the center to trigger a one-turn corner blackout for your opponent.
  • • Corner cells darken and cannot be selected while shadowed.
  • • The shadow clears right after the blocked player moves.
  • • Standard win rules: three in a row claims the light.
